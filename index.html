Intersection Congestion Pattern and Traffic Bottleneck Identification in Cities

Data Analytics & Algorithm Exploration

Goal

Exploring congestion patterns, mapping the spatial distribution of congested nodes and identifying the traffic bottlenecks can lead to targeted optimization

Approach

Splitting the congested intersections into different categories according to the congested traffic direction, road type, degree of congestion etc.

Explore

Exploring the spatial characters of all the congested intersections in a city so that we can identify the main traffic bottlenecks

Insights From Data

The project is focusing on data analytics and visualization upon the dataset of intersection congestions in four major cities (Chicago, Atlanta, Boston and Philadelphia) in US.

We propose that the congestion patterns are described by two dimensions: the degree of congestion and the difference in congestion time among driving routes.
